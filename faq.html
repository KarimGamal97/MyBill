Frequently Asked Questions

  • What are Mybill offers?

    Mybill allows participating stores to offer cashback offers of a percentage of your total purchases. This information is indicated in the offer.

  • What purchase invoice is accepted in the cashback process?

    The accepted invoice is the one that contains the barcode approved by the Zakat and Tax Authority for the electronic invoice Sometimes, the cashier will only give you the purchase receipt. Unfortunately, this is not considered acceptable. Ask the cashier for a detailed purchase invoice that contains a clear barcode so that you can get cash back on your purchases

  • What is a referral bonus?

    Referral bonus is a way through which you can get rewarded for inviting new users to Myble. A referral bonus is a great way to earn more cash and reach your profit goals faster.

  • What are Rules for earning a referral bonus ?

    1- You should invite a new friend to Mabel. A new user cannot create a second account just to use your referral code.
    2- The new friend must use his own account and receive a refund from the offer

  • What is a referral code?

    A referral code is a unique code that is assigned to you at the time of registration. Use this code to refer others and get a cash reward when they submit an invoice for their first purchase. The code can be shared with friends and family. The more new friends you invite to Maple, the more cash bonus you can get! The new friend must enter your code, and enter it correctly when registering in order to receive the referral bonus

  • When do I get referral cash for referring a new friend?

    You will receive referral cash for inviting the new friend to Maple when the new friend redeems their first offer.
