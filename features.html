Mybill Features For stores and companies

No More Purchase

No more paying money for marketing campaigns with no guaranteed results With Maple, you will not pay anything until the results are achieved and customers come to buy from your store or company.

Boosting Merchant Bonuses

Linking user accounts to stores Maple offers the option to link users' accounts with participating retailers. By doing this, the merchant can get a bonus from all the offers automatically received by the users linked to him.

Streamlined Integration

There is no need for technical connection between Maple and the stores and companies participating in the application Thus saving on the costs of allocating devices to operate the system or the costs of training employees to use it
